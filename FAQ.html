FAQs


        1. What inspired you to start this food blog?

        The story of Epicurean Harbour began with a shared love for the world's diverse cuisines 
        and the desire to bring the flavors of the globe closer to every enthusiast's kitchen. As
        avid travelers, food lovers, and curious souls, we embarked on a culinary adventure 
        that transcends borders and cultures.

        2. Do you test all the recipes yourself?

        At Epicurean Harbour, we take pride in ensuring that each recipe we share is crafted with care 
        and authenticity. While we don't personally test every recipe ourselves, our dedicated team 
        of experienced cooks meticulously researches, selects, and adapts recipes from around the 
        world. We believe in presenting recipes that capture the essence of their origins while 
        being accessible and achievable for our readers. Our commitment to quality means that 
        we rely on a combination of expert knowledge, thorough research, and the feedback from our 
        culinary community to curate a collection of recipes you can trust and enjoy with confidence.


        3.How do you choose which recipes to feature on your blog?

        Selecting the perfect recipes to feature on Epicurean Harbour is a meticulous process that
         balances culinary authenticity and widespread appeal. Our team of passionate food enthusiasts scours the globe to discover a 
         diverse range of dishes that highlight the 
         essence of different cuisines. We consider factors such as cultural significance, 
         uniqueness, and the accessibility of ingredients and techniques. Each chosen recipe 
         undergoes thorough testing and adaptation to ensure it aligns with our commitment to 
         providing reliable and delightful culinary experiences for our readers, offering them a 
         chance to explore the world's flavors from the 
         comfort of their own kitchens.
         
         4.Do you have any tips for taking better food photos?

         To capture captivating food photos, focus on lighting, composition, and presentation. 
         Utilize natural light whenever possible, avoiding harsh shadows. Experiment with angles
          to showcase textures and layers, and use the rule of thirds for balanced composition. 
          Enhance your shot with vibrant colors and garnishes that complement the dish. Attention 
          to detail and a clean backdrop can elevate the visual appeal of your food, inviting 
          viewers to savor the experience before even taking a bite.
